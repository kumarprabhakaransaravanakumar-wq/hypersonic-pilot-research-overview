# Research Boundary

This public repository intentionally contains only a research overview.

## Public

- project abstract and motivation;
- conceptual architecture;
- high-level physical grounding;
- selected aggregate evaluation results;
- limitations and research roadmap.

## Private

- implementation source code;
- trained checkpoints and model parameters;
- raw training and evaluation logs;
- detailed safety limits and runtime configuration;
- credentials, infrastructure, and deployment procedures;
- unpublished experiments and simulator modifications.

The separation allows public discussion and discoverability without presenting
an experimental controller as deployable flight software.
